Bobby the Lamp

Projects

A big part of who I am is building things. Ever since I was young I have always had a knack for taking things apart and putting them back together. This knack for understanding how things work has helped pave the way for my passion to create. Throughout the years I have gradually improved moving from simple electronics in my childhood to more advanced software-driven microcomputers such as Arduino and Raspberry Pi in my teen years. Some of the projects that I enjoy working on the most involve coding via Raspberry Pi, Arduino, Microsoft VBA, HTML & CSS, and JavaScript. I also enjoy woodworking, 3D printing, and graphic design.
